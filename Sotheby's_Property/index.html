Further Lane to the Ocean

Rare unobstructed Further-Lane-to-Atlantic-Ocean 7 acre compound with absolute total privacy: High-style published 10,000 sq ft oceanfront mansion by noted designer John Saladino with inland barn guest house on sweeping parcel of open farmland (subdivided to 2 lots) with 220 feet direct oceanfront in prime East Hampton estate location. Premium quality custom design uses old brick, hand-hewn beams, imported stone floors, plaster walls and custom cabinetry. Dramatic triple-height entry, oversized 20 by 30 ft beamed living room with 18 ft ceilings, gourmet kitchen with professional appliances, connoisseur wine tasting room, luxury primary suite, unobstructed waterviews from every room. Large waterside terrace with wisteria-shaded dining pergola, fireplace, 55 ft heated pool, and private path to white sand beach. Inland 3350 sq ft authentic 19th century barn guest house (expandable to 10,000 sq ft) with oversized recreation room, open kitchen/dining, 2 bedroom suites, 3-car garage
